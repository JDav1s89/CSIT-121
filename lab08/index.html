Email Form Input

Email forms are best used to ensure you are capturing email addresses in the proper format e.g. email@address.com

Scrolling Text Box

Scrolling Text Boxes are best used to provide a larger amount of space for users to enter in text such as for comments or questions.

Date Input

A Date Input is useful when requiring the user enter in a date such as for entering their birthday, setting the date of an appointment, or any other event that requires a specific month, day, or year be selected.
