Easy, Breezy, Cheesy Stuffed Bread (Domino's Copycat Recipe)

Submitted by Shannon Stone at AllRecipes.com

Go to Recipe

  • 1 (11 ounce) package refrigerated French bred dough
  • 4 tbsp melted butter, divided
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 11 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pinch dried parsley
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch onion powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a large baking sheet
  2. Roll dough into a large rectangle. Brush with 3 tbspn melted butter and sprinkle with cornmeal. Flip dough over and scatter mozzarella cheese, Mexican cheese blend, and 1 cup Cheddar cheese on top. Fold dough in half and press edges together with fingers to seal.
  3. Brush remaining 1 tbsp butter over dough and lightly sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and remaining 1 tbsp Cheddar cheese, then sprinkle with parsley, garlic powder, and onion powder. Make shallow cuts every inch across the top with a knife in order to easily pull bread apart after baking.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until bread is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 25 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes before pulling apart.

This is my favorite recipe because I absolutely love Domino's Stuffed Feta and Spinach Cheesy Bread and so being able to make it at home by just substituting some of the ingredients is perfect for when I'm trying to save money from eating out.
